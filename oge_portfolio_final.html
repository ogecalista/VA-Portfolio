Case Study

CRM Pipeline — From Chaos to Clarity

Amina Okoro had no system to track her leads. Names were scattered, follow-ups were missed, and hot prospects were going cold. I built a full CRM pipeline board in one week tracking 18 leads across 4 stages: New Leads → Contact Made → Proposal Sent → Won/Client. Every lead was tagged by priority (High, Medium, Upsell, Renew) with clear next actions assigned. A simple 10-minute weekly maintenance guide was also created so Amina runs the system herself independently.
